TRADITIONAL SKETCHES

Most of my life I've been actually doing traditional art. I've tried many mediums, but I find pen, pencils and alcohol markers most comfortable. I currently use Ohuhu brush markers and recmmend them to everyone. They're much cheaper and actually better than most of the mainstream brands.
